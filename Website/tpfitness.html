TPFitness

My Leadership Experience

As someone who is into fitness, this role meant a lot to me. TPFitness is an interest group where students train together to reach their fitness goals. However, due to Covid-19 it was a big challenge for my batch to bring the CCA together like the Pre-Covid days. Our aim was to bridge the students and the gym together by creating a community to train together and help one another when in need. Thus, We held weekly online training sessions and shifted to physical sessions as soon as the government eases the safety measures.

Organizing

All in all, We managed to host an event called Temasek Strength Classic (Powerlifting event) whereby students get to showcase their strength and bond together in a cost free competition setting! The most tedius part of making this event happen was the proposing of the event and the event flow planning, but thankfully I had the best Exco members to push through this event together!
